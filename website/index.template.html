Security

Your box. Your keys.
Nothing in the middle.

Conduit is built so the app on your phone and the agent on your machine talk directly over a connection you own — there's no Conduit server sitting in between holding your code or your credentials.

It's your connection

The app reaches your machine over your own SSH login. Your SSH keys are entered on-device and never leave it — and we never see your servers.

Not exposed to the internet

On a paired box the broker listens on localhost and is reached only through your authenticated, encrypted tunnel — not on a public port for the world to find.

Token-gated access

Every connection is authenticated with a high-entropy key minted when you pair the box. No key, no entry — checked in constant time on every request.

Encrypted on your machine

Agent credentials are encrypted at rest with a key that stays on your box — readable by the broker, not by a stray backup or a snooping process.

We don't hold your data

Sessions, code, and chat live on your box and your device. Push notifications travel through a relay that carries opaque tokens — never your code, keys, or messages.

Verified installs

The broker is delivered over HTTPS and checksum-verified before it ever runs, so a tampered or man-in-the-middled binary won't install on your server.

We treat the server you trust us to run with care — hardened by default, audited as we grow, and patched fast. Found something? Responsible disclosure is always welcome.
